DAILY NAPPING ROUTINE

This application creates a discipline in daily napping routine. It makes you more powerful, especially because it allows you to be more productive and energetic. This way you buy yourself more time to enjoy your day just the way you want to!

Power nap increases quality of time spent at work and during your private time. By taking a nap one increases and renews its energy levels and is able to perform better at work and still feel fresh for the rest of the day. These 30 minutes can have a major impact on the levels of success and it can lower the feeling of tiredness and lack of concentration.

Application is a very simple, easy to use alarm which allows one to choose time from 1-30 minutes waking up with custom made calming sounds. Because of its very clean and elegant design it is so simple to use, leaving no time for anything else but napping.
